The Student Government Association (SGA) recognizes a number of various student organizations. On top of providing funding for each club, SGA provides materials and supplies for clubs to use in their meetings, events, and promotions. In the Group Activities Center, the room behind SGA, there are a number of art supplies like markers, posters, stencils and more. The Information Desk, downstairs of the Dining Commons next to the Hill, also provides some storage for club supplies such as a Wii console that Gaming club uses.

If there are any questions regarding SGA Inventory, please contact SGA Executive Secretary Jerry Penaflorida at: jpenaflorida@students.stonehill.edu
